General

Where are courses held?

All courses are held at Oakland Digital's 1,300 square foot computer facility located at 1224 Harrison St. in downtown Oakland.

Students

What is the time commitment like?

Courses are one-day sessions that range from 1 to 5 hours in length.

What are the fees?

Free to $75, sliding scale and scholarships available. Call 510-435-2945 for more details.

Teachers

What is the time commitment like?

We recommend teachers course sessions that are about 2 hours long, but they can range from 1 to 5 hours in length.

What are the fees?

Oakland Digital is fueled by the passion and expertise of our volunteer-driven team. We suggest teachers donate 25% of their class fee to Oakland Digital to help keep our programs going strong.
