For Teachers

By teaching courses with Oakland Digital, teachers are helping underserved communities achieve economic success and empowerment through digital literacy. Oakland Digital's small business development initiatives and professional development programs for design students are making a difference, but we need your help to reach even more people in the community. Whether you're a creative director at an ad agency or a software engineer at a tech company, your knowledge will have an immediate impact.

About Oakland Digital

Oakland Digital Arts & Literacy Center is a 501(c)(3) community building organization working to bridge the digital literacy and opportunity divide. Since 2009, Oakland Digital has been working to educate, inspire and empower small business owners, local design students, and entrepreneurs.
