What is BRIDGE?

BRIDGE is an education platform that partners with tech and design professionals from the tech and design industry to offer courses that teach practical technical skills. We envision a future where under resourced communities have access to professional opportunities. BRIDGE aims to empower people with digital literacy education that will improve their lives and the communities they live in. Whether it's learning how to manage your business' brand through social media or code your first website, empower yourself to harness the the digital tools to succeed in the 21st century.
